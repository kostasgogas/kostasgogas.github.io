About Shop

I am a Multidisciplinary Artist

Working on original visual, literary, motion & sound works Nea Filadelfeia, Athens, Greece

I'm self-disruptor, breaking always free from the status-quo, not fearing to reinvent myself, stop the process, completely abandon what yesterday was my holy cup, change my views, don't care to answer social expectations or limit myself in any way. Working tirelessly on my own journey, which is one of development and trusting all my impulses - yet I have succeed in producing enormous amounts of work in multiple art forms. I was born in Ioannina, Greece in April 18th, 1987 and I was raised in Nea Filadelfeia, Athens, Greece from the age of 1, where I still live today.

Some people need to go on vacation to feel relaxed and restore their balance, I myself find me in a complete chaotic state of mind when I don't create, I must do it, either I like it or not it's the only thing that pulls me up from the ground, and I constantly need to remind myself that this is there waiting for me when I need it. And I need it a lot.

One thing I could never tolerate was 'behind the scenes' filtering, so at age 25 I decided that I will no longer leave anyone else decide what gets released and what not. This has become the most important thing for the way I work: independence, I do everything myself or I work closely with people that trust my vision and will support me when I'm down or when I'm up. I work slowly until my vision is achieved and I never let anyone hurry me, a true work of beauty is worth more that a million mediocre ones. That's why I always strive for perfection, even if that means that the majority of the hundreds of my projects will be left unfinished.
