Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying art from our online shop.

How do you deliver the artwork?

We use Elta, the standard shipping method in Greece.

How many days till my artwork reaches me?

Usually, it takes between 6-15 days.

15 days have passed but I haven't received my art yet.

If you haven’t received you art 15 days after you received the order confirmation email, then don’t hesitate to contact us.

What is your Return Policy?

If you really feel that your artwork wasn’t what you expected it to be, we can help. The artwork must be in the same condition and packaged the same way you received it. After receiving your artwork, you have 14 days to request a refund and ship the item back. Unfortunately the shipping costs should be covered by you.

I don't have PayPal, can I still buy?

Of course, having a PayPal account is optional. You can add your credit or debit card info all within the PayPal interface without having or creating an account with them.

Can I checkout as a guest?

Yes.

Where is your shop located?

We are based in Greece, in Nea Filadelfeia.

Is the shop really owned by the artist himself?

Yes, and your order will probably be fulfilled by artist Kostas Gogas when you buy an art piece.

What difference does it make buying directly from the artist?

First of all, you get originality of vision. Artist Kostas Gogas first creates the artwork, and then figures out a way to sell it. Most galleries do exactly the opposite: they see what sells and then they commision the artist for similar works. Kostas Gogas peculiar way of working, with the multitude of styles and reinventing his own self time and again is an artist that need freedom to create as he sees fit. Apart from that, getting rid of intermediaries and gallery representation allows us to offer the artworks in half the prices. And you get to communicate with someone that is happy you like his original work, and doesn’t care only for you wallet.

Can I visit a physical store?

Unfortunately no, but you can send us a message to arrange a meeting, if you want to see the artworks from upclose. Send us a message here.
